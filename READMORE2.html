BIKE WAREHOUSE

The Recycle & Reuse team pick up bicycle donations from our various drop-off sites throughout the county, diverting thousands of pounds from the waste stream each year, and getting bikes back on the road. The warehouse is open every other Saturday to the public to shop for parts & “as-is” bikes, and every other Saturday for adults to use our tools with the help of a professional mechanic.
